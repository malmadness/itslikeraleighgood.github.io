It's Like Raleigh Good

Hello Internet! Is the lack of styling on this page killing you? Me too.

Let me explain..

For the last few months (or more accurately, sporadically throughout the last year), I have been attempting to improve my html and css skills while also learning JavaScript. The hardest part about this has been investing time in a project that I don’t give a shit about. Sure it’s exciting when you figure out how to make a little cartoon man jump onto a platform that you added to a game, but also who the fuck cares?

I was a food blogger in a previous life and I ended up quitting for a lot of reasons. For one, I got way too obsessed with tracking website traffic and attracting followers. I stared blogging because I enjoyed being creative in the kitchen and sharing my learnings along they way, not because I cared how many people viewed a blog post. Creating new content became a chore and I stopped enjoying the process.

Another huge reason I quit blogging was because I knew close to nothing about web development. I never felt like I could get my blog to look or feel the way I wanted it to and I had no idea to fix it. At the time, I was still pretty new to my career as a Quality Engineer, working for a government contractor testing several websites that collected data behind the scenes of HealthCare.gov. Well fast forward seven years, I feel like I am definitely more than capable of building a website with some practice!

SO all of that is to say my blog is a work in progress. I’m posting this first entry because I plan to share the evolution of my blog as it progresses while I learn over the next few months. What am I going to blog about? Guess you’ll have to come back to see!
